Welcome to Starling Publications


We are a small press publisher specializing in the selling of limited edition horror and science fiction/fantasy books. Works published by Starling are aimed at the discriminating collectors and dedicated fans of the aforementioned genres.

By basing its main interface with authors online, Starling hopes to foster talent in these genres that may have been otherwise overlooked by traditional publishing outlets.
