About Us

Comfort Foods is an attempt to capture those foods that evoke emotion and bring back good memories. Our menus have been chosen by a polling of customers and is constantly updated and adjusted based on feedback by customers. Customers are also welcome to do substitutions to tweak meals so they are more to their liking. We also offering catering for corporate dinners, birthday parties and kiddie events.
